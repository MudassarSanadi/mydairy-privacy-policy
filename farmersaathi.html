Privacy Policy

FarmerSaathi respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles user data.

1. App Description

FarmerSaathi is designed to help milk dairies and farmers record milk deliveries, track 10-day billing, and manage advance payments. The app is intended for authorized users operating compatible devices.

2. Information Collection and Use

FarmerSaathi stores data related to milk entries, billing summaries, and advance payments locally on your device.

We do not collect or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Some technical data (such as device identifiers) may be used to ensure the app functions correctly, but no personal information is transmitted externally without your consent.

3. Data Storage

All operational data is stored locally on your device. No remote servers are used to store your farmer or milk records.

4. Permissions

FarmerSaathi may request certain device permissions, including:

  • Storage Access – To save and load milk records and billing data on your device.

These permissions are used strictly for the functionality of the app and are not for tracking or advertising purposes.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent user data to third parties.

No personal information is shared with advertisers or analytics providers.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to ensure that your local data is stored securely on your device.

7. Children’s Privacy

FarmerSaathi is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
