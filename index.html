Privacy Policy

MyDairy respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data.

Information Collection and Use

MyDairy does not collect or share personal information with third parties.

The app may store user-entered diary content locally on the device.

If users choose to attach photos to diary entries, the app accesses device storage only for this purpose.

Data Storage

All diary data is stored locally on the user’s device. We do not transmit data to any external servers.

Permissions

MyDairy uses photo and storage permissions only to allow users to attach images to diary entries.

Children’s Privacy

MyDairy is not designed for children under the age of 13.
