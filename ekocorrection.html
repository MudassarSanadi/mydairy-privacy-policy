Privacy Policy

EkoCorrection ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles user data.

1. App Description

EkoCorrection is designed to perform remote correction and configuration for the Eko Milk Ultra Pro Analyzer device. The app is intended for authorized users operating compatible analyzer hardware.

2. Information Collection and Use

EkoCorrection does not collect, store, or share personal information with third parties.

The app may process device-related technical data (such as analyzer configuration data) solely for the purpose of performing correction and calibration functions.

No personal user data is transmitted to external servers unless explicitly required for technical support and initiated by the user.

3. Data Storage

All operational data processed by the app is handled locally on the user’s device or directly communicated with the connected analyzer hardware.

We do not maintain remote servers for storing user data.

4. Permissions

EkoCorrection may request certain device permissions such as:

  • Bluetooth / USB / Network Access – To communicate with the Eko Milk Ultra Pro Analyzer device.
  • Storage Access (if applicable) – To save or load correction files locally on the device.

These permissions are used strictly for the functionality of the app and not for tracking or data collection purposes.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent user data to third parties.

No personal information is shared with advertisers or analytics providers.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to ensure that communication between the app and analyzer device is secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

EkoCorrection is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
