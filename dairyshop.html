Privacy Policy

DairyShop respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information from users of our app.

Information Collection and Use

DairyShop collects only the information necessary to allow dealers to place and manage orders, including:

  • Dealer name and contact information
  • Order details (products, quantities, and delivery instructions)
  • Device information for app functionality and troubleshooting

We do not share your personal information with third parties except for order processing or delivery purposes.

Data Storage

All order and dealer data is stored securely on the device and/or on our backend servers for processing and record-keeping. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data.

Permissions

DairyShop may request access to device storage or photos if you attach images with your orders. These permissions are only used for this functionality and are not used for other purposes.

Children’s Privacy

DairyShop is not intended for use by children under 13 years old.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. Users will be notified of significant changes in the app or via email if applicable.
